# 💠 [SYSTEM MANUAL: CORE VITALS] 💠

This document outlines the operational mechanics of the **XP**, **MP**, and **HP** systems within the Selfup AI ecosystem. These vitals represent the bridge between your real-world actions and your digital ascension.

---

## 📈 XP (Experience Points)
*The measure of your accumulated growth and the catalyst for Rank Ascension.*

### 1.1 The XP Loop
XP is acquired by completing tasks, habits, workouts, and quests. Unlike static systems, Selfup XP feeds directly into your **System Rank**.

### 1.2 Rank Thresholds
| Rank | Level Range | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rank E** | 1 - 9 | Awakened |
| **Rank D** | 10 - 19 | Pathfinder |
| **Rank C** | 20 - 29 | Vanguard |
| **Rank B** | 30 - 39 | Elite |
| **Rank A** | 40 - 49 | Master |
| **Rank S** | 50 | Ascendant |
| **Rank SSS** | ?? | System Architect |

### 1.3 XP Rewards (Sample)
- **Daily Habit**: +10 XP
- **High Priority Task**: +25 XP
- **Workout Session**: +50 XP
- **Weekly Quest**: +250 XP

---

## 🧪 MP (Mana Points)
*The spiritual energy required to execute advanced System Skills and AI interactions.*

### 2.1 The Energy Pool
MP is no longer a decorative value. It is a finite daily resource that dictates how much "System assistance" you can leverage.

### 2.2 System Skills (MP Consumption)
| Skill | Cost | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Auto-Sync** | 20 MP | AI automatically blocks your schedule for the next 24 hours. |
| **Cognitive Probe** | 35 MP | AI performs a deep-memory analysis to find hidden patterns in your productivity. |
| **Quest Shift** | 15 MP | Re-roll a Daily Quest that doesn't align with your current environment. |
| **Vessel Scan** | 10 MP | AI analyzes nutrition/fitness data to provide an immediate form correction or macro tip. |

### 2.3 Regeneration & Scaling
- **Base Max MP**: 100
- **Scaling**: Each point in **Intelligence (INT)** increases Max MP by +10.
- **Regeneration**: Base regen is 5% per hour. Each point in **Agility (AGI)** increases regen speed by +0.5% per hour.

---

## 💓 HP (Health Points)
*The integrity of your "Vessel." HP represents your consistency and physical well-being.*

### 3.1 The Integrity Guard
HP is the ultimate check against burnout and inconsistency. It is maintained through discipline.

### 3.2 HP Damage (Missed Habits)
- **Missed Daily Habit**: -5 HP
- **Broken Streak (7-day)**: -20 HP
- **Extended Inactivity (>48h)**: -50 HP

### 3.3 Recovery & Scaling
- **Base Max HP**: 100
- **Scaling**: Each point in **Vitality (VIT)** increases Max HP by +15.
- **Recovery**: HP is restored by completing "Recovery Tasks" or maintaining a "Perfect Day" (100% habit completion).

---

## 🛡️ Attributes & Vitals Correlation

The **System** calculates your capabilities based on your attribute distribution:

| Attribute | Primary Vital | Secondary Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength (STR)** | **XP Multiplier** | Increases XP gain from physical (Fitness) tasks. |
| **Intelligence (INT)** | **Max MP** | Unlocks higher-tier "System Skills." |
| **Agility (AGI)** | **MP Regen** | Shortens the cooldown between AI interactions. |
| **Vitality (VIT)** | **Max HP** | Increases the "error margin" before a streak reset penalty. |

---

> [!CAUTION]
> **SYSTEM WARNING**: If HP reaches 0, the user will enter a **"System Lockdown"** state. XP gain is halved and AI interactions are restricted until a "Recovery Quest" is completed.

> [!TIP]
> **STRATEGY**: Early-game pathfinders should focus on **Intelligence** to increase MP capacity, allowing for more frequent AI scheduling, which in turn drives faster XP accumulation.
